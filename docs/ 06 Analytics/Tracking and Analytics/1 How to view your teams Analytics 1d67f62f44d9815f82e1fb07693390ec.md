# How to view your teams Analytics?

To view analytics for your team, follow these steps- 

1. Head over to the side bar and hover over the first icon. 

![IMG_0704.jpeg](How%20to%20view%20your%20teams%20Analytics/IMG_0704.jpeg)

1. You will see two options, Getting Started and Analytics. 

![IMG_0705.jpeg](How%20to%20view%20your%20teams%20Analytics/IMG_0705.jpeg)

1. Click on Analytics. 

![image.png](How%20to%20view%20your%20teams%20Analytics/image.png)

1. There should be a default dashboard there, but if not then please create one by clicking the dropdown, and then pressing the create dashboard button. 

![IMG_0706.jpeg](How%20to%20view%20your%20teams%20Analytics/IMG_0706.jpeg)
