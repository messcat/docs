# How to use Notes?


Notes are a powerful feature inside the inbox to create quick notes for your or your teams reference. The note appears in the chat but is not sent to the recipient, it is visible to your teammates as well. 

![notes.jpeg](How%20to%20use%20Notes/c8f375ca-c203-41a2-a5b3-28c22e3ace33.png)

### Use Case

**Notes** allow you and your team members to add comments on a specific contact.

### Other features

- **Note-taking**: Allows adding notes to a specific contact.
- **Privacy**: Notes are only visible to you and your team members.
- **Team** **Collaboration**: Enables tagging of team members in the notes.
- **Mark as Resolved**: Option to mark notes as 'resolved' when issues are addressed.

## Related FAQ & Articles

### 1. When my teammates tag my username in note, why is it displayed with ID instead of username?

- You should be directly tagging your teammates username **without comma** after the username.
- Please make sure the **Admin** of your team has enabled the access of “Read the members of your team” for your role.
- Please re-login the account and try again after complete the access.
