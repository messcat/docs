# How to send a Message in Messcat?

### Steps

### **Access Your Inbox**

- On the Messcat app, tap on the **Inbox** tab located on the left screen.

![Untitled](How%20to%20send%20a%20Message%20in%20Messcat/Untitled.png)

### **Select a Chat**

- If you manage multiple business channels , you might see a list of channels on the filter section. In this case, tap on the desired **channel** and **select** a chat to start messaging with customers.

### **Start Interacting with Your Customers**

- Once you've accessed the inbox or selected the appropriate channel, you can now compose and send messages to your customers directly.
