# How to Change the Assignee of a Contact

You can change the assignee of a contact in Messcat using three different methods:

## **Through the Inbox**

![20241216-170327.jpeg](How%20to%20Change%20the%20Assignee%20of%20a%20Contact/20241216-170327.jpeg)

1. **Locate the Contact**: Open the **Inbox** and find the contact you want to reassign.
2. **Assign Button**: Click on the **“Assign”** button at the bottom-right corner of the contact’s details.
    1. If the contact is already assigned, this button will display the name of the current assignee.
3. **Select Teammate**: Choose the teammate to whom you want to assign the chat.

---

## **Through the Contacts Page**

![20241216-170544.jpeg](How%20to%20Change%20the%20Assignee%20of%20a%20Contact/20241216-170544.jpeg)

1. **Navigate to Contacts**: Go to the **Contacts** page and locate the desired contact.
2. **Update Assignee**: In the contact’s details, find the **“Assignee”** section and select a new teammate to reassign the contact.

---

## **Through Message Flows**

![Screenshot 2024-12-16 at 5.15.08 PM.png](How%20to%20Change%20the%20Assignee%20of%20a%20Contact/Screenshot_2024-12-16_at_5.15.08_PM.png)

1. **Automate Assignments**: Use message flows to automatically reassign contacts.
2. **Action Blocks**: Add an **“[Action](https://help.messcat.ai/message-flows/kSYFmwLcqbtLQQAhJb4Sek/how-to-use-action-node/2hMwkGyPQrEx4kGdQVftyv)”** or **“[Modify Contact](https://help.messcat.ai/message-flows/kSYFmwLcqbtLQQAhJb4Sek/how-to-use-the-modify-contact-node/3gRYWsiT6aJ6wUxBEQUaUq)”** block within the flow to change the contact’s assignee based on predefined conditions.
