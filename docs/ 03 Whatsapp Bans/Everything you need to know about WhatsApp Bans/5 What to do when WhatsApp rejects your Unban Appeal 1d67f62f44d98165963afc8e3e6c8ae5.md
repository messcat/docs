# What to do when WhatsApp rejects your Unban Appeal?

Article Description: Life after getting banned. 


There is not much that can be done to get your Account back if WhatsApp rejects your unban appeal. This could happen because of repeated violations. 
You can start fresh with another number, or multiple new numbers. 
In some cases whatsapp may unban your account after a certain amount of time but this is not the norm and may not happen.
