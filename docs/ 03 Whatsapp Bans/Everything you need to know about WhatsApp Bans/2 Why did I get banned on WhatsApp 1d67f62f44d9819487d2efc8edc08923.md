# Why did I get banned on WhatsApp?

Article Description: Common reasons for getting Banned on Whatsapp.


There can be multiple reasons for why your account got banned on WhatsApp. It usually happens when you violate one of their Policies. 
WhatsApp may ban accounts without prior warning, particularly if an account is suspected of posing a risk to the community. 

The most popular reasons for a whatsapp ban are – 

1. Getting Blocked by multiple numbers for spamming them. 
2. Sending messages to new numbers(numbers which you do not have a prior chat history with)
3. Broadcasting too often at very high speeds to unknown numbers. 

***It is always advisable to adhere to WhatsApp's guidelines and use the platform responsibly to avoid such actions against one's account.***
