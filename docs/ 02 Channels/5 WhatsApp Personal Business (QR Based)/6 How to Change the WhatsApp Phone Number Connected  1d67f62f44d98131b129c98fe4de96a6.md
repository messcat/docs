# How to Change the WhatsApp Phone Number Connected to a Channel


Changing the WhatsApp phone number connected to a channel in Messcat is a straightforward process. Follow the steps below to ensure a seamless transition:

**Step 1**: Login to Messcat with your Phone Number and Password

**Step 2**: On the Messcat dashboard, hover over the menu on the left-hand side. Find the “**Inbox**” section and the click “**Manage Channels**”

![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.35.45 PM.png](../The%20Basics/How%20to%20auto-assign%20chats/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.35.45_PM.png)

**Step 3**: Locate the channel that you want to change the number for and click “Scan QR”

![20250131-163724.jpeg](Seamlessly%20Reconnecting%20After%20a%20Long%20Time%20Migratin/20250131-163724.jpeg)

**Step 4**: You will be prompted to disconnect your current phone number. Click “OK”.

![20250131-170335.jpeg](Seamlessly%20Reconnecting%20After%20a%20Long%20Time%20Migratin/20250131-170335.jpeg)

Step 5: Scan the QR with your new phone number and you have your new channel connected!

![Screenshot 2025-01-27 at 3.25.56 PM.png](Seamlessly%20Reconnecting%20After%20a%20Long%20Time%20Migratin/Screenshot_2025-01-27_at_3.25.56_PM.png)
