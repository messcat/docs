## Introduction

This guide will show you how to connect your Facebook Messenger to Messcat so you can manage your messages all in one place.

## **Before You Start**

- You will only be able to receive and send messages through the Instagram channel **if you have a page connected to your Facebook account**.

### Steps

**Step 1: Go to Manage Channels**

- Open your Messcat account
- Find "Manage Channels" in the side menu
- Click the "+ Channel" button
- Select "Messenger"
    
    ![image.png](Messenger%20Channel/image.png)
    

**Step 2: Log in to Facebook**

- A new window will open
- Sign in with your Facebook username and password

**Step 3: Choose Your Pages**

- Facebook will show you available Business Pages to connect
- Select which pages you want to manage through Messcat

**Step 4: Review and Save**

- Review the permissions Messcat is requesting
- Check all **permissions** carefully
- If everything looks correct, click "Save"

**Step 5: You're Connected!**

- You'll receive confirmation that your Messenger Channel is ready
- Congratulations! You're all set! ðŸ¥³

## **Important Things to Know**

- **Business Account Only:** Remember, you need a *Business* account, not a personal one.
- **Linked to Facebook:** Your Messenger account *must* be connected to a Facebook Page.

- **The Conversation Window:** Messenger has a rule that says:
    - You can message someone freely within 24 hours after they messaged you.
    - After 24 hours, you can only message them with a manually sent message. You must only use this to follow up on an issue, or provide assistance that couldnâ€™t have been given in the first 24 hours. This window lasts for 7 days.
- **Old Messages:** Messages that existed *before* connecting to Messcat will not appear in Messcat.
- **Using Meta Business Suite:** If you use the Meta Business Suite to message people, those messages might not appear in Messcat. It's best to use Messcat for your Messenger messages after you connect.
- **File Size Limits:**
    - Pictures: 8MB limit
    - Other Files: 25MB limit

## Help! I'm Having Trouble!

- **Channel Connection Problems:**
    - Make sure you're logged in to the right Facebook account.
    - Double-check that your Facebook Business account is linked to a Facebook Page.
    - Verify that you've granted all necessary permissions to Messcat on the Manage Channel page.
- **Message Problems:**
    - Manage your messages only in Messcat to avoid sync issues with other platforms like Meta Business Suite.
- **Billing:**
    - If you're redirected to the payment page, your account has run out of credits.
