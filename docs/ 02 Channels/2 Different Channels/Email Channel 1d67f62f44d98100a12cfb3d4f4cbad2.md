# Intro

Elevate Your Marketing Strategy with Messcat's brand-new email integration. You can now seamlessly integrate your email marketing efforts with your overall communication strategy.

# Steps

## **Access Manage Channels**

- Click on your user icon in the top right corner of the Messcat dashboard or head over to inbox from the side panel. Then, select “**Manage Channels**”

![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.35.45 PM.png](../The%20Basics/How%20to%20auto-assign%20chats/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.35.45_PM.png)

## **Add a New Channel**

- Click the **Add Channel** button.
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 4.25.17 PM.png](Email%20Channel/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_4.25.17_PM.png)
    

## **Choose Email Channel**

- Select **Email** from the available channel options.
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 4.25.46 PM.png](Email%20Channel/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_4.25.46_PM.png)
    

## **Configure Email Channel**

- Ensure you use a private domain email address associated with your company name.
    - For example [info@messcat.ai](mailto:info@messcat.ai)
    - Public domain addresses like [gmail.com](http://gmail.com) won't work.
        
        ![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 4.26.34 PM.png](Email%20Channel/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_4.26.34_PM.png)
        

## Confirm your email ID and **Start Sending Emails**

- You will be receiving an email from Postman to confirm your email ID
- Once you've confirmed and configured the email channel, you should be able to send emails through Messcat.
