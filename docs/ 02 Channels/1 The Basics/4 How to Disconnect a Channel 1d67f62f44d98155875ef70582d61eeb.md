# How to Disconnect a Channel?


<aside>
‼️

If you disconnect a channel, you will not be able to send or receive any messages till you re-connect it again

</aside>

Step 1: Click on your user icon in the top right corner of the Messcat dashboard or head over to inbox from the side panel. Then, select “**Manage Channels**”

![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.35.45 PM.png](How%20to%20auto-assign%20chats/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.35.45_PM.png)

Step 2: Click the **three dot menu** on the top right of the channel you wish to disconnect and then select “Disconnect”
