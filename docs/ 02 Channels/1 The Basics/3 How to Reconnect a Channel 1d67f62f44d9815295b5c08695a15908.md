# How to Reconnect a Channel?


Your Channels can sometimes get disconnected, follow the steps below to reconnect a channel. 

**Step 1. Access Manage Channels**

- On the Messcat dashboard, hover over the menu on the left-hand side. Find the “**Inbox**” section and the click “**Manage Channels**”
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.35.45 PM.png](How%20to%20auto-assign%20chats/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.35.45_PM.png)
    

**Step 2. Identify Disconnected Channel**

- Within the **Manage Channels** section, locate the specific channel that appears as disconnected.
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.36.37 PM.png](How%20to%20Reconnect%20a%20Channel/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.36.37_PM.png)
    

**Step 3. Initiate Reconnection**

- Click the **Scan QR** button associated with the disconnected channel.

**Step 4. Choose Reconnection Method**

- You'll be presented with two options to reconnect the channel:
    - **Scan QR from WhatsApp:** Open the WhatsApp app on your phone and scan the QR code displayed on your Messcat screen.
        
        ![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.37.14 PM.png](How%20to%20Reconnect%20a%20Channel/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.37.14_PM.png)
        
    - **Link with Phone Number:** Enter the phone number associated with the WhatsApp account you want to reconnect and enter the code provided on your WhatsApp Account.
        
        ![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.37.23 PM.png](How%20to%20Reconnect%20a%20Channel/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.37.23_PM.png)
