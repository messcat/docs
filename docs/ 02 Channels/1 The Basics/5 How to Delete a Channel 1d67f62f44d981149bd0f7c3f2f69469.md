# How to Delete a Channel?

# Steps

<aside>
❗

You can only delete channels if you have an **admin role** and the channel has never been connected before. Channels that have the “**DISCONNECTED**” indicator cannot be deleted

</aside>

<aside>
❗

If you wish to delete a channel that has been connected before, please reach out to our customer support team

</aside>

<aside>
❗

Deleting a channel will remove all contacts, messages, chats, notes, and assignees related to that channel. This cannot be undone

</aside>

1. Go to the **user icon** on the top right corner, then click **Manage Channels**.

![Screenshot 2025-01-31 at 12.35.45 PM.png](How%20to%20auto-assign%20chats/Screenshot_2025-01-31_at_12.35.45_PM.png)

1. Click on the 3 dots on the channel you wish to delete, and click **Delete**.
