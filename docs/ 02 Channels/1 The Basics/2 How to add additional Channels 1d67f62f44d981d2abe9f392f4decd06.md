# How to add additional Channels?

[[https://drive.google.com/file/d/1GYsH-AQJCeSBFXapmOrG09_-VkYbTv47/view?usp=sharing](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1GYsH-AQJCeSBFXapmOrG09_-VkYbTv47/view?usp=sharing)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1GYsH-AQJCeSBFXapmOrG09_-VkYbTv47/view?usp=sharing)

**Step 1: Access Manage Channels**

Click on your user icon in the top right corner of the Messcat dashboard or head over to inbox from the side panel.

- Select **Manage Channels**

**Step 2: Add a New Channel**

Click on the **+ Channel** button in the bottom right

**Step 3: Connect Your New Channel**

Connect the desired channel by following the steps on the screen. You might have to scan a qr, fill in an OTP or go through the Whatsapp Business API setup process to do this. 

**Verify Connection**

Once you've completed the connection steps, wait for the channel status to show **Connected**.

Once, thats done, you can start messaging in your new channel.
