# How to Set Up Payments on Facebook Business Manager for MessCat WABA Account

This guide will walk you through the steps to set up payment settings on Facebook Business Manager (FBM) for your MesscatWhatsApp Business API (WABA) account.

[[https://drive.google.com/file/d/19viYXDOygn5dO8SYkeOOHGvqDilpAj_0/view?usp=drive_link](https://drive.google.com/file/d/19viYXDOygn5dO8SYkeOOHGvqDilpAj_0/view?usp=drive_link)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/19viYXDOygn5dO8SYkeOOHGvqDilpAj_0/view?usp=drive_link)

### Step 1: Log in to Facebook Business Manager

1. Go to Facebook Business Manager.
2. Log in using your credentials.

### Step 2: Access Business Settings

1. From the main dashboard, click on the Settings icon in the top right corner.

### Step 3: Navigate to WhatsApp Account

1. Go to Accounts > WhatsApp Accounts.
2. Select the WABA account under Messcat, click on "Payment Setting"

### Step 4: Add Payment Method

1. Click the Add Payment Method button.
2. Choose your preferred payment method (e.g., Credit/Debit Card, PayPal).
3. Enter the necessary payment details.
4. Click Continue and review the information.
5. Once confirmed, click Save to add the payment method.

### Step 5: Confirm Successful Setup

1. Check your email for a confirmation from Facebook about your payment setup.
2. You can also check the Billing section in Facebook Business Manager for any updates or invoices related to your WABA account.
