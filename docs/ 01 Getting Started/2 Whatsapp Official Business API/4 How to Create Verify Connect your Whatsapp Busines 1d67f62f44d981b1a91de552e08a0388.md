# How to Create/Verify/Connect your Whatsapp Business API to Messcat?


<aside>
❗ Before you can Create/Verify/Connect your Whatsapp Business API to Messcat, please make sure you have a Meta Business Account with a Verified Business on Meta. 

If not, then please refer to the [previous help doc](https://Messcat.helpkit.so/getting-started/922MY2B1hVzdtQHBsTicEx/how-to-get-a-meta-business-manager-account-and-verify-your-business-on-meta-required-for-official-whatsapp-business-api/sh9NGxsCXjQimj9HbeyG7e).

</aside>

Please note, by applying for **Whatsapp Business API with Messcat**, you acknowledge and agree to the conversation charges. (Merchants using the WhatsApp Business platform are charged per conversation, which includes all messages delivered within a 24-hour conversation period.) For your reference, please find the conversation rate table [here](https://www.notion.so/6d30f0d226db426c9aa12dade21dbd16?pvs=21). Before using the service, please review the rates carefully and let us know if you have any questions or concerns.

## Steps

1. Login to [your Messcataccount](https://app.messcat.ai/) 
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-13 at 5.27.08 PM.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Screenshot_2025-01-13_at_5.27.08_PM.png)
    
2. Hover over the left-hand sidebar to find the “**Inbox**” section. Then, click on “**Manage Channels**”
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-13 at 5.27.44 PM.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Screenshot_2025-01-13_at_5.27.44_PM.png)
    
3. Click on the “**+ Add Channel**” button on the top right, then select “**WhatsApp Business API(WABA)**”
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-13 at 6.40.35 PM.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Screenshot_2025-01-13_at_6.40.35_PM.png)
    
4. Login to your **Meta Admin Account**
    
    <aside>
    ❗
    
    Ensure you use the account that manages access to the company’s Facebook Meta Business settings
    
    </aside>
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-13 at 6.41.59 PM.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Screenshot_2025-01-13_at_6.41.59_PM.png)
    
5. After logging in, click **Get Started** 
    
    ![Feb Payslip.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Feb_Payslip.png)
    
6. Select your existing Meta Business Account from the “**Business portfolio**” dropdown and click “**Next**”
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-13 at 6.48.14 PM.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Screenshot_2025-01-13_at_6.48.14_PM.png)
    
7. Choose your WABA account and select whether you want to:
    - Create a new WhatsApp Business Account, or
    - Use an existing phone number.
    
    ![20250114-104830.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/20250114-104830.png)
    

<aside>
❗

**Note:** If the number is linked to an existing WhatsApp Business app account, you must delete the account using the app, wait 3-4 minutes, and then continue.
    1. Please be aware that:
        ▪ The number will start afresh.
        ▪ All chats will be lost.
        ▪ You won’t receive messages until the API request is approved.

</aside>

1. Enter your **WhatsApp business account name, display name, and category**. Then, click “**Next**” 
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-13 at 6.53.17 PM.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Screenshot_2025-01-13_at_6.53.17_PM.png)
    
2. Enter the phone number you want to use for WABA, and once you obtain the OTP via the registered phone number, enter it to complete the WABA application
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-01-13 at 6.56.32 PM.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/Screenshot_2025-01-13_at_6.56.32_PM.png)
    
3. **Update Payment Details:**
    - After the application, ensure you **add a payment card on Meta** to activate the WABA channel successfully and enable messaging.
    - Please refer to [this help doc](https://help.messcat.ai/getting-started/922MY2B1hVzdtQHBsTicEx/how-to-set-up-payments-on-facebook-business-manager-for-Messcat-waba-account/ioUNadacYnUF7gHaqzWx9t) for detailed steps regarding How to Set Up Payments on Facebook Business Manager.
4. **Check WABA Approval Status:**
    - The Channel Card will display your WABA details with a **Pending Approval** status.
    - Click **Refresh WABA Details** to check the latest status.
        
        ![20250113-190457.jpeg](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/20250113-190457.jpeg)
        
        - If it says **Approved**, your WABA application has been approved.
            
            ![20250113-190307.png](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/20250113-190307.png)
            
        - If it says **Rejected/Pending**, you may need to relink your Meta account with Messcat. Please contact customer support by clicking here to open the WABA service.
    
    <aside>
    ❗
    
    **Note:** Approval status updates usually take 1-2 days.
    
    </aside>
    

1. Once approved, update your profile picture and description directly on the Channel Card under the **Manage Channels** page.
    
    ![20250113-190636.jpeg](How%20to%20Create%20Verify%20Connect%20your%20Whatsapp%20Busines%201d67-0388/20250113-190636.jpeg)
