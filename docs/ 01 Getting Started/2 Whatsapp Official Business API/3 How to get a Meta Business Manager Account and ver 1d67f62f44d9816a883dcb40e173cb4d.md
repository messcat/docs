To start business account verification, ensure eligibility and control over the account. If the "Start verification" button is not visible in the Security Centre, check if verification is required for desired features, based on any received notifications or emails. 

Misrepresenting information or unauthorised claims during verification can lead to denial of verification, removal of verified status, or additional actions by Meta.

1. Navigate to the Security Centre in Business Manager and click "Start Verification."
2. Enter your business details and click "Next."
3. Choose your business from the provided list or select "None of these match" if not listed.
4. Select a verification method: Phone call, Text Message, Email Verification, or Domain Verification.
    1. For Phone or Text, provide a document with your organization's name and phone number.
    2. Email Verification is the quickest method.
    3. For Domain Verification, use an already verified domain or verify your domain and then continue.
5. Upload documents to confirm your Business Name & Address.
    1. **Documents required for Facebook Business Verification**
    You'll need to provide one of the following documents to verify your business: 
        1. **Organizations.** If you're a registered business or organization, please provide one of the following:
            1. **Government-issued company or corporate registration**.
            2. **Company tax revenue document** (for example, a corporate tax return or tax-exempt certificate).
            3. **Official articles of incorporation or association**.
        2. **Sole proprietors** If you're a registered sole proprietor and operate your business by yourself, please submit one of the document types listed below:
            1. **A government registration document**. This document must confirm that you are a sole proprietor.
            2. **Company tax filing document**.
        3. **If you're not a registered business or sole proprietor, please submit the requested ID below:**
            1. **Proof of ID**. This must be a [valid government-issued identification document](https://www.facebook.com/help/245465555858336) that includes a photograph, preferably a passport.
            2. **Proof of address**. Note that your document must clearly show the address you registered with Facebook, must be in effect, and must be no more than 6 months old. Acceptable proof can include you:
                1. Utility bill.
                2. Bank statement.
                3. Credit card statement.
                4. Company tax filing documents.

## FAQ

1. **How to check if your business is verified?**
    
    ![How to check if business is verified (1).gif](How%20to%20get%20a%20Meta%20Business%20Manager%20Account%20and%20ver/How_to_check_if_business_is_verified_(1).gif)
    
    1. Log into your Facebook Business Manager account and click on 'Business Settings'.
    2. Select 'Security Center'.
    3. View the verification status displayed.
2. **What if the 'Start Verification' button is greyed?**
Meta may prevent your business from being verified if the submission does not meet the requirements for verification. This could include:
    1. Submitting, or being suspected of submitting false or misleading information
    2. Attempting to claim or verify a business account you don't own or have authorization to represent
    3. Trying to circumvent Meta's verification review system
    4. Having a website that fails to load, is not HTTPS compliant, or contains a link that leads to an error message
    
    If your business verification application was rejected, you may be eligible to appeal. Visit the Security Centre or return to where you began the verification process to learn more about the status of your verification.
    
3. **After I submitted my business details, I saw a list of business records. What should I choose if none of the businesses listed match?**
If META can find a public record that matches your business details, you will see a list of business records. You may either select the correct record from the list or select **My business isn't listed** or **None of these matches.**
4. **Do I need to submit documents if I use monthly invoicing to pay for ads?**
If you use monthly invoicing to pay for ads, you don't need to submit documentation for business verification. When you start the business verification process, you can fill in the form with your line of credit information and submit it. If you can't see that option, make sure that the business account you're trying to verify is linked to a credit line that you own, not one that is shared with another business.
5. **How long do I need to wait to know the verification decision after submission?**
After you've submitted your verification, a decision can be made in as little as 10 minutes and as long as 14 working days. You'll receive a notification when the review is complete. If you receive confirmation that your business is verified, there's nothing more you need to do.
