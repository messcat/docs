# How to switch teams in MessCat
If you have multiple teams in Messcat, you can switch between them by following these steps:

![Untitled (2).png](How%20to%20switch%20teams%20in%20MessCat/Untitled_(2).png)

Step 1: On your Messcatpage, on the top right corner, click the User Icon.

Step 2: Click **Switch Team**. Then, switch to team by using the team name or the correct Team ID.
