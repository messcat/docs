**Q: How to Move Around in the Message Flow?**

A. Navigating the message flow is easy and can be done in multiple ways depending on your preferred device:

**1. Using a Trackpad**

**Pinch to Zoom**: Zoom in and out using the pinch gesture.

**Two-Finger Drag**: Move around the canvas by dragging with two fingers.

**Tap to Select**: Tap on a node or element to select it.

**2. Using a Mouse + Keyboard**

- **Scroll Wheel**:
    - Scroll up and down using the mouse's scroll wheel.
    - Hold **Shift** + Scroll Wheel to move left and right.
- **Middle Mouse Button**:
    - Press and hold the scroll wheel, then drag to move around.
- **Space + Left Click + Drag**: Hold the **Space** key, then left-click and drag to pan around the flow.
- **Zooming**:
    - Hold **Command** (Mac) or **Ctrl** (Windows) + Scroll Wheel to zoom in and out.
