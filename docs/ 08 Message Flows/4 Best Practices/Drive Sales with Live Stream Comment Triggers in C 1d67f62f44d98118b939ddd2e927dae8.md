# Drive Sales with Live Stream Comment Triggers in Messcat


## Intro

This guide focuses on using Messcat to automate lead generation and offer exclusive deals during your live streams, maximizing conversions in real-time.

## **Use Case: Generating Leads and Offering Exclusive Deals**

- **Goal:** Offer a limited-time discount or freebie to live viewers who comment with a specific keyword.
- **Benefit:** Drive immediate conversions, generate leads, and create a sense of urgency.

![image.png](Drive%20Sales%20with%20Live%20Stream%20Comment%20Triggers%20in%20C/image.png)

## **Streamline Your Implementation**

### **1. Craft Your Winning Offer**

- Define your compelling offer that delivers real value to viewers - whether it's a discount code, downloadable guide, or exclusive webinar access.
- Select an engaging, clear keyword that resonates with your audience and stands out in comments (e.g., "DEAL," "FREEBIE," "GIFT").

### **2. Set Up Your Messcat Automation**

- **Trigger:**
    - Launch a new "Facebook/Instagram Comment" trigger.
    - Configure the trigger to respond to your chosen keyword (e.g., "DEAL").
    - Enable the "Live Video Status" is "Live" condition.
    
    ![image.png](Drive%20Sales%20with%20Live%20Stream%20Comment%20Triggers%20in%20C/image%201.png)
    
- **Action:**
    - Add a "Send Message" action.
    - Craft a private message (DM) that includes:
        - A brief description of the offer.
        - The discount code, download link, or sign-up instructions.
        - A clear call to action.
        - Example: "Here is your 20% discount code! Use CODE20 at checkout. [Link to your website]"
- **Optional Action:**
    - Add "Add Tag" action.
    - Tag the user with something like "LiveDeal" to track them.
    
    ![image.png](Drive%20Sales%20with%20Live%20Stream%20Comment%20Triggers%20in%20C/image%202.png)
    

### **3. Announce the Offer During Your Live Stream**

- Clearly explain the offer and the keyword to your viewers.
- Encourage them to comment with the keyword to receive the exclusive deal.
- Example: "Comment 'DEAL' now, and I will DM you an exclusive 20% discount code!"

### **4. Monitor and Engage**

- Monitor the live chat for comments containing your keyword.
- Ensure that Messcat is sending the automated DMs correctly.
- Engage with viewers who have claimed the offer and answer any questions.

### **5. Follow Up (Optional)**

- If you have tagged the user, you can create a follow up broadcast to this tagged user.
- Send a reminder about the offer or provide additional resources.

## **Key Considerations**

- **Clarity:** Make the offer and keyword easy to understand.
- **Urgency:** Create a sense of urgency by emphasizing the limited-time nature of the offer.
- **Value:** Ensure the offer is valuable and relevant to your audience.
- **Testing:** Thoroughly test your automation before going live.
- **Transparency:** Let users know they will receive a DM when commenting.

By following these steps, you can effectively use Messcat to generate leads and drive sales during your live streams.
