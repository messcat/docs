### Using the Broadcast Trigger

The Broadcast trigger facilitates sending broadcast messages to multiple contacts simultaneously. This trigger allows you to efficiently communicate important updates, promotions, or announcements to a large audience, ensuring timely and consistent messaging across your contact base.
