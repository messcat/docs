# How to Tag a contact in Inbox?

You have two option to add a tag inside “Inbox” page:

### How to add tag in conversation logs:

1. Go to your **Inbox**.
2. Open a specific **conversation log**.
3. Select **"+ Add Tag"** and choose the desired tag(s) to assign to the contact.

![Untitled](How%20to%20Tag%20a%20contact%20in%20Inbox/Untitled.png)

---

### **How to add tag from Inbox Sidebar (Contact Info):**

1. Access your **Inbox**.
2. In the conversation list sidebar, locate the “arrow” to expand the menu and navigate to **Contact Info**.
    
    ![Untitled](How%20to%20Tag%20a%20contact%20in%20Inbox/Untitled%201.png)
    
3. Click on the contact information section to expand it.
4. Within the contact details, find the **"Tags"** section.
5. Click **"+ Add Tag"** to assign a new tag or select an existing tag.
