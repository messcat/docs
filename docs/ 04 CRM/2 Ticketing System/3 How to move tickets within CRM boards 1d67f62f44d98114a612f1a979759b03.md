# How to move tickets within CRM boards

You have three options for updating a ticket stage:

---

### **Option 1: Update Ticket Stage from the CRM Page**

[[https://drive.google.com/file/d/1dd6eV6DeuhwXE-4wfjLVyzfOsK_ANfZX/view?usp=drive_link](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1dd6eV6DeuhwXE-4wfjLVyzfOsK_ANfZX/view?usp=drive_link)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1dd6eV6DeuhwXE-4wfjLVyzfOsK_ANfZX/view?usp=drive_link)

1. Navigate to the **CRM Page**.
2. Locate the ticket whose stage you want to update.
3. Click on the **current stage** of the ticket to open its details.
4. In the ticket details popup, find the **stage dropdown** at the top right corner.
5. Select the new stage you want to move the ticket to.
6. The ticket will be updated and moved to the selected stage.

### **Option 2: Update Ticket Stage from the Inbox Page**

[[https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xYqzsNDXsT2McVxwfj5FOQPO_Hsffv8V/view?usp=sharing](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xYqzsNDXsT2McVxwfj5FOQPO_Hsffv8V/view?usp=sharing)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xYqzsNDXsT2McVxwfj5FOQPO_Hsffv8V/view?usp=sharing)

1. Navigate to the **Inbox Page**.
2. Open the **Contact Info Sidebar** on the right-hand side.
3. Locate the **Tickets Section** and click on the ticket you want to update.
4. In the ticket details, select the new stage you want to move the ticket to.
5. The ticket will be updated and moved to the selected stage.

<aside>
ðŸ’¡

**Tip**: Similar to the CRM page, you can move tickets to a different CRM board directly from the ticket details.

</aside>

---

### **Option 3: Update Ticket Stage Using Triggers and App Nodes in Message Flows**

1. Use **triggers** and **app nodes** in your message flows to automate the ticket stage update process.
2. For a step-by-step guide, refer to the [documentation on using triggers and app nodes](https://help.Messcat.tech/message-flows/kSYFmwLcqbtLQQAhJb4Sek/how-to-use-the-update-ticket-node/wdSHptD6CreTaneMXs78Hc).

### **Why Use This Feature?**

Easily manage and organize tickets across different stages and boards within your CRM system for streamlined workflows and efficient task management.
