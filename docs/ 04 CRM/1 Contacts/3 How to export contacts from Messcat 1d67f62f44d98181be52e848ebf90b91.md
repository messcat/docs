# How to export contacts from Messcat


Exporting contacts from Messcat simplifies contact list management by allowing users to extract their contacts, along with associated tags, custom fields, and field data for external use. 

# Steps

## **1. Accessing Your Contacts**

- On the Messcat dashboard, locate the navigation menu on the left side of the page.
- Find the **Contact Management** section and click **Contacts** Click on that section.

## **2. Selecting Contacts for Export**

- You'll see a list of all your contacts within the Messcat account.
- There is a checkbox in the top left corner of the list. Clicking this checkbox will select all contacts for export at once. This is the quickest option if you want to export all your contacts.
    
    ![2024-05-09 12_43_54-Window.png](How%20to%20export%20contacts%20from%20Messcat/2024-05-09_12_43_54-Window.png)
    
- Alternatively, if you only want to export specific contacts, you can individually select them by clicking the checkbox next to each desired contact name.

## **3. Exporting the Contacts**

- Once you've chosen the contacts you want to export (either all or specific ones), locate the **Export** button.
- Click the **Export** button. Messcat will download the contacts in a CSV format for export.
