# How to enable Push Notifications on Browser?

## **Access Notification Settings**

- Open the Messcat app.
- Look for "Settings" and “Notifications” section within the app.

## **Manage Section Notifications**

- You'll see a list of different sections within the app that can send notifications.
- Each section have its own toggle or settings to enable/disable notifications or customize their behavior.
- The app allows selecting the delivery method for notifications, you'll see options like "Email" or "Push.”

## **Enable Notifications (if applicable)**

- Enabling notifications involve granting permission through the device's notification settings. In this case, you might see a prompt to "Allow Notifications" when you try to enable them.
    - If you encounter this prompt, tap on "Allow" to grant the app permission to send notifications for that section.
    - Or you can click on the site settings menu to grant the app permission.
        
        ![notification.jpeg](How%20to%20enable%20Push%20Notifications%20on%20Browser/notification.jpeg)
        

## **Customize Notification Preferences (Optional)**

- Some sections offer options to further personalize notifications.
- Explore the notification settings within the specific section for these customization options.

<aside>
❗ **Note:** To enable notification for desktop, you also need to enable the notification in your browser and device.

</aside>

# Use Case

- Choose email or push notifications for different app sections and receive updates on what matters to you.
- Manage notification settings to receive alerts for important updates and silence the ones you don't need.
