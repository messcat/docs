# How to Invite Team members?



## Intro

Seamlessly expand your team by sending invitations to new members. Enhance collaboration and productivity with this straightforward tool. Let's explore how it simplifies team building!

## Steps

### **Access Team Invitation**

- Open the Messcat app and navigate to **Settings > Team** page.
- Locate the **"Invite Team Member"** option.

### **Select Role and Invite**

- Choose the appropriate role for the team member from the available options.
- You can then invite them in two ways:
    - **Direct Invite:** Enter their phone number and send an invitation directly through the app.
    - **Invitation Link:** Generate a unique invitation link and share it with the team member. They can then use this link to join your team.
