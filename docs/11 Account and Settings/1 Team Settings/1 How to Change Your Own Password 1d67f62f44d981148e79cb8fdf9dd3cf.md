# How to Change Your Own Password?

Last Updated: January 23, 2025


![image.png](How%20to%20Change%20Your%20Own%20Password/image.png)

Step 1: Go to "**Settings**" > "**Team**". 

Step 2: In the **Personal Information** tab at the **bottom right**, click "**Reset Password**".

Step 3: Create a new password and click "**Send OTP**".

Step 4: Verify the OTP to complete your password reset.
