# How to update Payment Information?

Step 1: Hover your cursor on the left navigation bar, then hover it to “**Settings**”, then click on “**Billing”.**

![de026dc5-9cb9-45fa-bcf5-a27eea2d08c1.png](How%20to%20update%20Payment%20Information/de026dc5-9cb9-45fa-bcf5-a27eea2d08c1.png)

Step 2: Click on Manage Billing. 

Step 3. After that click on “Update Information”.

![d54e2058-6336-4a11-aa0d-ba7b40ff9f4b.png](How%20to%20update%20Payment%20Information/d54e2058-6336-4a11-aa0d-ba7b40ff9f4b.png)

Step 4: Input your billing information, and then click on “save”.

![4c544f12-3e57-4dea-b670-f764ddee9305.png](How%20to%20update%20Payment%20Information/4c544f12-3e57-4dea-b670-f764ddee9305.png)
