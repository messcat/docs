# Billing FAQ

1. **What payment options does Messcat support?**
    1. Credit Card
    2. (Debit) Payment Card
    3. VISA, MasterCard, AMEX, UnionPay
2. **How do I know if my payment is successful?**
    
    If your payment has been successfully processed, please check your payment email as you should receive a notification via Email.
    
3. **When will I receive the invoice or receipt after the payment is done?**
    
    If your payment has been successfully processed, you should receive your **receipt** via Email immediately.
    
4. **Can I pay in installments?**
    
    No, we do not accept installments at the moment.
    
5. **How can I know about the next renewal subscription?**
    
    You will receive an email notification 7 days before renewal in your payment email inbox.
    
6. **When will my account** be activated **after payment is done?**
    
    When the payment is completed, your subscription will be reflected immediately once you re-login to your account.
