# How to Manage Billing?

Step 1: Hover your cursor on the left navigation bar, then hover it to “Settings”, then click on “Billing”.

![1280X1280 (18).PNG](How%20to%20Manage%20Billing/1280X1280_(18).png)

Step 2: Click on “Manage Billing”.

![1280X1280 (19).PNG](How%20to%20Manage%20Billing/1280X1280_(19).png)

Step 3: You can find the Payment Method, Billing and Shipping Information and Invoice History here.

![1280X1280 (20).PNG](How%20to%20Manage%20Billing/1280X1280_(20).png)
