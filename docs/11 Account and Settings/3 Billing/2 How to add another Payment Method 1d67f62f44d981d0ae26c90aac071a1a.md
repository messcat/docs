# How to add another Payment Method?

Step 1: Hover your cursor on the left navigation bar, then hover it to “**Settings**”, then click on “**Billing”.**

![1280X1280 (21).PNG](How%20to%20add%20another%20Payment%20Method/1280X1280_(21).png)

Step 2: Click on “Add payment method”.

![1280X1280 (22).PNG](How%20to%20add%20another%20Payment%20Method/1280X1280_(22).png)

Step 3:

Input your payment details, then click on “Add”.

![a8f98859-084e-4624-89eb-f8fdcb415da9.png](How%20to%20add%20another%20Payment%20Method/a8f98859-084e-4624-89eb-f8fdcb415da9.png)
