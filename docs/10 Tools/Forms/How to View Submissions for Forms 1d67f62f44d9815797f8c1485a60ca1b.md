# How to View Submissions for Forms?



Ever wondered what happens after someone submits your form? Here's where you can view all the data collected, allowing you to analyze responses, track progress, and gain valuable insights from your audience. This centralized view of submissions enables you to make the most of the information gathered through your forms.

# Steps

## **Accessing Form Submissions**

- In the Messcat dashboard, navigate to the menu on the left.
- Click on **Automation** followed by **Forms**.

## **Locating Your Form**

- Identify the specific form you want to view submissions for.

## **Viewing Submissions**

- Locate the **Submission** section within the chosen form.
- Click on **View Submissions** to access the collected data.

# Use Case

## **Organized Workflow**

- Manage applications effectively by having all submissions in one place, streamlining the selection process.

## **Efficient Review**

- Quickly compare and evaluate applications side-by-side to make informed decisions.

## **Improved Communication**

- Easily contact applicants directly through Messcat if you need further information or clarification.
