# How to setup AI Chatbot

## **What is an AI ChatBot?**

The **AI ChatBot** allows you to train a GPT-powered chatbot using company-specific data, such as websites, PDFs, or documents. Once trained, the chatbot can respond automatically to incoming queries or assist teammates by suggesting replies.

### **4. Configure ChatBot Settings**

- **ChatBot Name**: Set a nickname for your chatbot.
- **Prompt**: Provide specific instructions for how the chatbot should behave (e.g., tone, style).
- **Fallback Message**: Define the message the chatbot will send when it cannot answer a query.
- **Use as AI Assistant**: Enable this option to let the chatbot **suggest responses** while teammates reply to messages in the Inbox.

### **5. Link the ChatBot to a Channel**

- **Enable Auto Reply**:
    - Turn this ON to allow the chatbot to automatically respond to incoming queries.
    - Leave it OFF if you only want the chatbot to **suggest replies** instead of replying automatically.
- **Include Sources in Chatbot Response**:
    - Enable this option to include the **sources or links** used at the bottom of each chatbot response.
- **Select Channel**:
    - Choose a channel where the chatbot will respond to all incoming messages.
- **Select Teammate**:
    - Assign a teammate to handle queries when the chatbot cannot respond.
